# Marketplace

Browse and publish loops organized by category.

> **Status: Preview.** The marketplace is a Git-based index. No hosted
> service yet.

## Categories

Research · Coding · DevOps · Security · Compliance · Marketing · Sales ·
Legal · Healthcare · Finance · Infrastructure · Education

📖 **[Browse the marketplace →](../../marketplace/)**

## Submitting a loop

Each submission includes: Loopfile, diagram, benchmark, essay,
verification notes, replay notes, license, tags, difficulty, estimated
runtime, required capabilities.

See the [marketplace README](../../marketplace/README.md) for the
submission template.

## Principles

- Verification over popularity
- No fake numbers
- No paid placement
